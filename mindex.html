🟢 Who We Are

Marittika isn’t just a name; it’s a movement. Hi, I’m Soniya Kumari – an investment strategist, startup consultant, earthen cookware exporter, women’s empowerment advocate, and your friendly neighborhood impact-maker.

At Marittika, we operate at the intersection of craft, commerce, and community. We provide a platform for women artisans creating eco-friendly, handmade earthen cookware—reviving age-old traditions and giving them global homes (yes, we export too!). But Marittika isn’t just about beautiful pots and pans; it’s about purpose and possibility.

💼 What We Do

  • 🟡 Empower Women Artisans: We collaborate with skilled women across India who create handcrafted, sustainable cookware.
  • 🔵 Export Earthen Cookware: Yes, we ship worldwide! Our eco-conscious, health-friendly cookware is loved in kitchens across the globe.
  • 🟣 Startup & Brand Consultation: I offer brand strategy, business expansion consultation, and investor readiness guidance for startups.
  • 🔴 Social Impact Collaboration: We support women entrepreneurs and girl children through our collaboration with the Likhari Foundation.
